Why Join CLC?

CLC offers a way of life that integrates faith and daily living.

By joining, you become part of a global community dedicated to the service of the Church and the world. We support each other in growing spiritually and serving those in need.

Eligibility

  • ✅ Practicing Catholic
  • ✅ Willingness to follow the Ignatian way of life
  • ✅ Commitment to attend meetings and participate in activities
  • ✅ Age: 15 years and above (for Youth Wing)
Note: Membership involves an initial formation period before permanent commitment.
