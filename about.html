Our History, Purpose, and Mission

What is CLC?

A global community following Christ more closely.

The Christian Life Community (CLC) is an international association of Christians: men and women, adults and youth, of all social conditions, who want to follow Jesus Christ more closely and work with Him for the building of the Kingdom.
