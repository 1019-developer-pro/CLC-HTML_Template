Our History

From Sodality of Our Lady to Global Community

Rooted in the Spiritual Exercises of St. Ignatius of Loyola, our history dates back to 1563 with the founding of the Sodality of Our Lady by Jean Leunis, SJ. For over 450 years, this community has evolved into a global movement of laypeople committed to following Christ.

In 1967, the Sodalities transformed into the Christian Life Community (CLC), marking a shift towards a more integrated lay vocation. Today, CLC Koratty Forane continues this legacy, fostering spiritual growth and apostolic service in our local parishes.
